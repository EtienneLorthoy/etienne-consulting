## Professional positioning

Three strands define his current professional identity:

1. **Senior full-stack and performance engineer.** Experienced across the stack with a track record of architectural leadership, particularly the kind of work that pays back over years (decomposing monoliths, building solid foundations, mentoring teammates). Recommendations from peers describe him as solution-driven with a growth mindset, a stellar teammate, and one of the strongest developers they have worked with.

2. **Indie game developer and shipper.** Solo developer of **Hard Chip**, a 3D simulation and puzzle game about engineering an 8-bit processor from individual NAND and NOR gates upward. The game shipped to Steam Early Access on 2024-11-22 and is also distributed on itch.io. This is a technically uncompromising title (digital logic simulation, real-time 3D, custom rendering) that demonstrates depth in C# .NET, HLSL (High Level Shader Language) shader work, and end-to-end product execution.

3. **Independent consultant.** Operates as a freelancer focused on developing and modernizing code and IT infrastructure for clients, separate from his game work.
